Germany 🇩🇪

Germany is a country known for its rich history, culture, and architecture. Visitors can experience iconic landmarks such as the Brandenburg Gate in Berlin and fairytale castles of Bavaria. The country is also renowned for its traditional food and drinks, such as sausages, sauerkraut, and beer and Oktoberfest in Munich is a must experience.
