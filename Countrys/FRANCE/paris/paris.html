The city is known for its gastronomy, with a broad selection of restaurants serving classic French fare, as well as for its picturesque areas like Montmartre and stunning parks like the Luxembourg Gardens.

Paris is a fantastic place to go shopping since it has upscale stores and well-known fashion businesses. The ideal holiday spot in Paris is for couples, families, and art enthusiasts.
