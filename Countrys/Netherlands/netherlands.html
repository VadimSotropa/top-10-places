Netherlands 🇳🇱

Netherlands, also known as Holland, is a charming country filled with picturesque landscapes and traditional windmills. Famous for its colorful tulip fields and canals, it offers a unique and interesting experience for visitors. Cities like Amsterdam and The Hague are perfect to explore on a bike and discover the local culture, history and architecture. Don't forget to try the delicious Dutch cheese and stroopwafels. Come and see for yourself why Holland is such a friendly and inviting destination. Have a great trip!
